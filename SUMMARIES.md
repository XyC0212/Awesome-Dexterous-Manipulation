# Paper Summaries

One-sentence summaries used for retrieval, newest first. Full metadata — labels, venue,
year, affiliation, and links — lives in the [README table](README.md).

- **T-Rex: Tactile-Reactive Dexterous Manipulation** ([paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17055)) — T-Rex pairs a 100-hour tactile dataset with a variable-rate Mixture-of-Transformers and a temporal tactile encoder to learn tactile-reactive dexterous policies, beating the strongest baseline by over 30% on twelve precision force-control and deformable-object tasks.
- **SimToolReal: An Object-Centric Policy for Zero-Shot Dexterous Tool Manipulation** ([paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16863)) — SimToolReal trains a single object-centric RL policy over procedurally generated tool-like objects in simulation to achieve zero-shot dexterous tool use on a real 22-DoF five-fingered hand, outperforming retargeting and fixed-grasp baselines by 37% across 120 real-world rollouts.
