T-Rex pairs a 100-hour tactile dataset with a variable-rate Mixture-of-Transformers and a temporal tactile encoder to learn tactile-reactive dexterous policies, beating the strongest baseline by over 30% on twelve precision force-control and deformable-object tasks.
